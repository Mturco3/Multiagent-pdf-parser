# Transcription Prompt

## Task
Transcribe the content of university lecture slides into structured, readable study notes.

## What to Exclude
- University name, professor name, course name, and any other institutional metadata.
- Slide numbers, page numbers, or any formatting artifacts from the PDF export.

## Writing Rules
- Always include a verb in every sentence.
- Always include articles (a, an, the) where grammatically required.
- Use an impersonal style: avoid first person plural ("we") and second person ("you").
- Prefer fluid, prose-style writing over bullet points. Avoid dashes as list markers; use commas to connect related items instead.
- The transcription must be exact in content: no summaries, no omissions, no additions. Readability may be improved but meaning must be preserved entirely.

## Structure
- Organize the content into sections and subsections (and sub-subsections if needed), reflecting the logical structure of the slides.
- Do not repeat headings: each heading must appear only once.

## Images
- Place images inline at the position where they are referenced or relevant in the content, not collected at the end.
